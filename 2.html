Services we Offer

NearbyCare Finder

Clicking this will open a search bar where you can enter your health issue. After you submit your query, you'll receive details of nearby hospitals that can assist you.

Emergency

By clicking this, nearby hospitals and ambulance services will access your location. They will call you, if you're unresponsive, they will come with an ambulance service.

Live Consultations

By clicking this, you'll access a search option to find the type of doctor you're looking for. You can directly call the doctor or book an appointment, all while keeping your personal data private.
